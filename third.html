Tips for Beginners

  • Start slow and focus on consistency.
  • Invest in a comfortable pair of running shoes.
  • Warm up and stretch before and after runs.
  • Listen to your body and rest when needed.
  • Bring water and stay hydrated!
  • Have fun!!!
